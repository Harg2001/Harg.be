Frequently asked questions

Is it guaranteed?
No campaign guarantees an exact number of sales, but average rates allow for realistic expectations.

Example: 500 emails sent.
40 % open rate -> 200 people read the email
15 % click rate -> 30 visits to your store page
10 % purchase rate -> 3 sales
These are indicative figures, but realistic.

Using UTM parameters in links allows tracking email readers to obtain final campaign figures.

Can I stop after the test?
Yes. You are free at each step.

Do I have to write the emails?
Not necessarily. We handle the optimized writing.
We just ask for a game key and permission to use the game's graphic elements that we display in the campaign.
You have the possibility to submit your custom content, and we exchange with you to integrate it without compromising the quality of the campaign.

Do I have to provide the target audience?
No, the target audience is part of the offer.
If you have already made sales and/or run marketing campaigns elsewhere, we can exclude from our target players who have already bought your game or received a campaign from you.

Do I need to translate my entire game?
Not necessarily. A French store page improves results, but even partial localization can work depending on the genre.

Are there any restrictions?
We reserve the right to refuse campaigns for games with inappropriate content (extreme violence, explicit sexual content, hate speech, etc.) or for campaigns that do not respect best practices in email marketing (spam, misleading content, etc.).

We do not relaunch on any targets who have already received a campaign for the same game without having visited or purchased it.

Transparency & compliance

All campaigns are executed using professional email marketing infrastructure and follow industry standards and applicable data protection regulations.

The goal of this service is to connect interested players with new games, not to send unsolicited spam.
